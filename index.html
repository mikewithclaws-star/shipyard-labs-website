Who We Are

15 Years of Solving Business Problems with Technology

Shipyard Labs was founded by a strategic technology leader who has spent over 15 years turning complex business challenges into working software. With a career spanning Fortune 500 enterprises to scrappy startups, the mission has always been the same: make technology deliver real results.

Corporate AI Roadmaps & Digital Transformation

Our founder has managed corporate AI roadmaps and led individual AI initiatives at major organizations — including global staffing firms, commercial real estate enterprises, and technology companies.
